Graphic Design Experience

I have had the opportunty to work on Graphic Design both in and out of the classroom at the University of Miami. All works are done in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. Below are samples of that work.

Hijinks

For this project we were asked to create a new company to then create an overall design effect for. Works include Logo design, Stationary, Business Cards, Envelopes, Website Landing Page, and Company Packaging for temporary tattoos.

Publix Brohure

For this project we were prompted to design a new Publix Brochure. Depicted here is a 12-panel mockup with included bleed and trim marks. The "placemat" (bottom), once completely unfolded, provides fun games for the child. While the exterior (top) holds Nutrition Facts for the various cereals.

Cinematic Arts Commission & Hurricane Productions

With my time serving as the Chair of Cinematic Arts Commission and Executive Board Member of Hurricane Productions at the University of Miami, I was responsible for creating various promotional and advertising items for the organizations. Works include movie lineup cards, weekly advertisements, facebook banners, palmcards, posters, and t-shirts.

CAC Logo

In 2017, each branch of Hurricane Productions (7 organizations included) underwent a logo re-design. Some changes were minor, but the new CAC logo started from scratch. The Cinematic Arts Commission is a student run organization responsible for hosting free weekly movies for students on campus. I designed this logo which appears on all CAC platforms: social media, advertisements, signage, promotional items, etc.
